"Fucker Nuts 9000"

Created by Luke Soliman

Artists Description:

I was inspired to make this after getting my first colonoscopy, drawing inspiration from a painting I saw in the doctors office of a mangled house rat half submerged in a cup of coffee. The juxtaposition of sharp, pastel colors used to highlight the rat created such a jarring, bold tone I found so moving that I almost forgot I was having a colonoscopy.
